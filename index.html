about us

who we are

Bazza Football Academy, founded in November 2006, is situated in the vibrant city of Abuja, Nigeria. Our primary objective is to identify and develop young soccer talents not only from Abuja but also from across Nigeria and the broader African region. Committed to our mission of shaping these talents into individuals of character and excellence, we operate as a registered institution and proudly maintain affiliations with the Nigeria Football Federation and the Abuja Football Association.

Our Mission and Vision

What we do is what defines us

At Bazza Football Academy, our mission is to nurture the next generation of exceptional football talent by providing comprehensive training programs that foster skill development, sportsmanship, and personal growth. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment where players can excel both on and off the field, empowering them to reach their fullest potential as athletes and individuals.

Our vision at Bazza Football Academy is to become a renowned hub for football excellence, recognized globally for producing top-tier athletes who embody the values of dedication, teamwork, and integrity. Through innovative coaching methodologies and state-of-the-art facilities, we aspire to cultivate a legacy of success, inspiring aspiring footballers to dream big and achieve greatness in the beautiful game.
