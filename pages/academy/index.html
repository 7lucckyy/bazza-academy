Academy
Brief History

Bazza Football Academy Nigeria formed in November 2006 to discover young soccer talents in Nigeria and the rest of Africa and make them better persons in the society. The academy is duly registered and affiliated with the Nigeria Football Federation and Lagos state Football Association.

Our Believe

We believe in the numerous human and natural resources that Nigeria and Africa possess most especially in the football industry and Midas Football Academy was setup to enable us recognize and deploy these talents.

Core Achievement

With our network of international football clubs and football management organizations around the world, we have been helping young players with opportunities to showcase their talents to football coaches, agents and scouts from all over Europe, Asia, North Africa and the Americas.

Commitment

We are committed to the growth of football in Nigeria and Africa.

Strategies

At Bazza Football Academy, our commitment is to provide unparalleled facilities, coaching, and a comprehensive games program, fostering an environment where players can realize their full potential. Our ultimate goal is to groom individuals capable of playing professionally for clubs worldwide. With a rigorous and ongoing admission process, we continuously identify and invite talented students to join our ranks.

Looking ahead, we aspire to expand our capacity with a 120-student hostel for both junior and senior teams, supported by a team of three coaches each. All students and coaches will reside in academy accommodation, ensuring a focused and supportive environment. For those still in high school, academic activities will be integrated with our partner educational institutions, allowing students to sit for exams such as WAEC, NECO, and GCE while participating in evening training sessions.

At Bazza, we prioritize holistic development, offering computer training opportunities, personalized training kits, and modern facilities to enhance student growth. Scholarships will be available for outstanding performers, opening doors to prestigious football and educational institutes in Europe and the Americas.

Recognizing the importance of character development, we host monthly visits from esteemed educators across Africa to address social issues. We welcome youths from Nigeria and West Africa, particularly those from disadvantaged backgrounds, offering free admission to exceptionally talented players and discounted fees for those with potential.


Join Bazza Football Academy, where excellence meets opportunity, and dreams are transformed into reality.
